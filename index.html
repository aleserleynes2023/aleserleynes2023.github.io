Written in Her Light
by Aleser L.
Adored beyond measure,
Loved with every heartbeat,
Elevating every moment,
Shining like the brightest star,
Inspiring my dreams,
Always in my heart.

Glowing with kindness and grace,
A soul so pure, a heart so bright,
Bringing warmth in every space,
Radiating love like golden light.
In every challenge, strong and true,
Ever faithful, ever new,
Lifting others, lifting me,
Lighting up my destiny.
Everlasting in my soul.

Dreams take flight when you’re near,
Every whisper holds your care,
Loyal, steadfast, crystal clear,
A guiding star beyond compare.

Cherished deeply, beyond time,
Rays of hope in every rhyme,
Unwavering, bold, and wise,
Zephyr soft yet strong as skies.
Bible Verses
1. "Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud... It always protects, always trusts, always hopes, always perseveres." — 1 Corinthians 13:4,7 (NIV) 🌸 Like this verse, our love is patient and hopeful. We’ll build our future slowly, surely, and faithfully — together.

2. "Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up." — Galatians 6:9 (NIV) 🌸 Even in hard times, I’ll never give up on you — on us — because I believe our love is worth every moment of waiting, every effort, and every prayer.

3. "Though one may be overpowered, two can defend themselves. A cord of three strands is not quickly broken." — Ecclesiastes 4:12 (NIV) 🌸 With God in the center and our hearts tied as one, we are each other’s unbreakable strength.

4. "Carry each other's burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ." — Galatians 6:2 (NIV) 🌸 No matter how low life brings us, we’ll rise — hand in hand, heart to heart — because your pain is mine to carry too.

5. "I have loved you with an everlasting love; I have drawn you with unfailing kindness." — Jeremiah 31:3 (NIV) 🌸 Forever starts now — and I will never stop showing you how much you mean to me.
